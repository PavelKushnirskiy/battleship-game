Privacy Policy

Battleship from Childhood  ·  by KUSHNIRSKIY sp z o.o.

✅ COPPA Compliant — Children Under 13

This Privacy Policy describes how KUSHNIRSKIY sp z o.o. ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game Battleship from Childhood (the "App"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

🛡️
We do NOT collect any personal information.
Battleship from Childhood works fully offline and does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

📋 Information We Collect

We do not collect any information from users of this App. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, videos, or microphone data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any personal information from children under 13

👶 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our App is designed for children and we take their privacy very seriously. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
  • We do not require registration or account creation to play the game.
  • We do not display third-party advertisements.
  • We do not include any social features, chat, or user-generated content.
  • The App functions entirely offline with no internet connection required.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected information from your child, please contact us immediately using the information below and we will delete it promptly.

📡 Internet & Third-Party Services

Battleship from Childhood does not require an internet connection and does not connect to any servers, third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

No data is sent to or received from any external service at any time.

🔒 Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, there is no personal data at risk. The App stores only local game progress on your device (such as scores), which never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents & Guardians

We encourage parents to be involved in their children's digital activities. This App was designed to be a safe, fun, and completely private experience. You can let your child play with full confidence that:

  • No personal data is ever collected
  • No account or sign-up is needed
  • No in-app purchases are required
  • No ads are shown
  • No internet access is used during gameplay
